Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how RWAToday.news collects, uses, and handles information when you visit our site. We keep this simple because we keep it minimal.

What We Collect

RWAToday.news does not collect personal information from visitors beyond what is standard for any web server. We do not require account creation, login, or personal data submission to read our content. Our newsletter signup (via Beehiiv) collects your email address if you choose to subscribe — that data is governed by Beehiiv's privacy policy, not ours.

Analytics

We use standard web analytics to understand how our content is being read — which articles are most visited, how long readers stay, what devices they use. This data is aggregated and anonymized. We do not track individual users across sessions or across other websites.

Cookies

Our site may use minimal functional cookies to support basic site operations. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or cross-site behavioral profiling. If you have a browser configured to block all cookies, the site will continue to function normally.

Third-Party Links

RWAToday.news links to external sites including CoinMarketCap, Ondo Finance, and others. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Review their policies separately before providing any personal information.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page with a new date. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
