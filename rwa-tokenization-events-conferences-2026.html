Where the RWA Industry Is Meeting in 2026: The Complete Conference and Events Guide

From dedicated tokenization summits to crypto mega-conferences with RWA stages to agtech and real estate events where blockchain is showing up for the first time — the complete guide to where the real-world asset industry is gathering in 2026.

📅 2026-06-10⏱ 8 min read🔄 Updated regularly

The RWA and tokenization conference circuit has exploded in 2026. Three years ago there were a handful of blockchain events with a "tokenization panel." Now there are dedicated RWA summits, tokenization-only conferences, and major industry events across agriculture, real estate, commodities, and capital markets that have added blockchain and tokenization tracks to their agendas.

This guide covers the events worth knowing — from exclusively RWA-focused gatherings to the large crypto conferences with strong tokenization programming to the industry vertical events where RWA is showing up in unexpected places. We'll update this as new events are announced.

Dedicated RWA and Tokenization Events

These events are built specifically around real-world asset tokenization — no generalist crypto programming, just practitioners working on the problem.

RWA DEDICATED
TokenizeThis 2026
June 23–25, 2026 · The Glasshouse, New York City
The world's longest-running conference dedicated exclusively to the tokenization of finance. Under RedStone's leadership in 2026, the lineup includes Fidelity, Apollo, Franklin Templeton, WisdomTree, and Securitize. Institutional tracks cover stablecoins, stocks, private credit, funds, repos, and structured products. A full RWA Innovation Expo runs alongside.
→ RWA angle: Institutional-grade programming with real allocators and issuers in the room
→ tokenizethis.redstone.finance/
RWA DEDICATED
RWA Summit — Global Series
Dubai (May 1, 2026 ✅ completed) · Hong Kong (2026) · Singapore (2026 TBC)
The world's premier dedicated RWA summit series. Each edition brings together regulators, institutional investors, asset managers, and blockchain infrastructure teams. The Dubai edition attracted 1,000+ attendees and 200+ investors. The Hong Kong and Singapore editions follow throughout the year.
→ RWA angle: The go-to series for sovereign and institutional tokenization discussions across Asia and MENA
→ rwasummit.global/
RWA DEDICATED
RWA Day NYC 2026
May 12, 2026 ✅ completed · Chelsea Arts Tower, New York
A curated, invite-only 100-seat forum for allocators, fund managers, and infrastructure teams. No speaker showcases — practitioner-led roundtables with Chatham House-style candor. Real numbers, real deal structures. Built for decision-makers, not spectators. Now in its second year.
→ RWA angle: The highest signal-to-noise ratio RWA event in North America
→ cryptoevents.global/rwa-day-nyc-2026/
TOKENIZATION
TOK26 — London Tokenisation Summit
January 29, 2026 ✅ completed · London
Second annual edition of the UK's leading tokenisation summit, convening policymakers, regulators, institutional investors, and digital finance innovators. Strong focus on regulatory frameworks, scaling infrastructure, and cross-border coordination. A key European event for the institutional side of the sector.
→ RWA angle: UK and European regulatory landscape, MiCA implications, institutional adoption pace
→ crypto.news/event/tok26-the-london-tokenisation-summit-2026/
INSTITUTIONAL
London Blockchain Summit — Institutional Tokenisation
July 7, 2026 · DLA Piper, 160 Aldersgate, London
A focused institutional forum at DLA Piper's London office, convening buy side, sell side, and infrastructure teams. Announced June 10, 2026. Confirmed speakers include Laser Digital (Nomura), Franklin Templeton Digital Assets, Baillie Gifford, and AMINA Bank. Reflects the global coordination now required as tokenized markets operate around the clock.
→ RWA angle: Franklin Templeton, Nomura, and institutional buy side in one room discussing operational tokenization

Major Crypto Conferences With Strong RWA Programming

These are the large-tent crypto events where tokenization is a significant track — not the whole show, but substantive enough that RWA practitioners attend specifically for those sessions.

CRYPTO MAJOR
Consensus Miami 2026
May 5–7, 2026 ✅ completed · Miami Beach Convention Center
CoinDesk's flagship conference drew 20,000+ attendees and 500+ speakers across 6 stages. The Tokenization track covered institutional adoption, stablecoin infrastructure, and RWA composability in DeFi. A second Consensus Hong Kong edition ran in February. The largest crypto event in the Americas.
→ RWA angle: Tokenization, stablecoins, and payments track — one of 6 content stages
→ consensus2026.coindesk.com/
CRYPTO MAJOR
ETHDenver 2026
February–March 2026 ✅ completed · Denver, Colorado (annual)
The world's largest Ethereum hackathon and conference — 30,000+ attendees, builders-first culture, and increasingly significant RWA programming as DeFi/RWA composability has become a primary sector question. Chainlink, Aave, and the oracle/infrastructure teams whose work underpins RWA protocols are all heavily represented. ETHDenver is where the technical architecture decisions for next-generation RWA systems get debated, prototyped, and stress-tested. Mark the next edition for February 2027.
→ RWA angle: DeFi/RWA composability, smart contract infrastructure, Chainlink oracle work, on-chain lending collateral
→ ethdenver.com
CRYPTO MAJOR
TOKEN2049 Singapore 2026
October 7–8, 2026 · Marina Bay Sands, Singapore
25,000+ attendees, 300+ speakers, 500+ exhibitors. TOKEN2049 Week runs October 5–11, with side events surrounding the main conference. Asia's dominant crypto event and the meeting place for institutional capital allocating into the sector. RWA consistently features as a top-five topic at TOKEN2049 given Singapore's regulatory leadership in tokenization.
→ RWA angle: Institutional adoption, DeFi/RWA integration, Asian regulatory environment
→ www.token2049.com/singapore
CRYPTO MAJOR
Paris Blockchain Week 2026
April 15–16, 2026 ✅ completed · Paris
Europe's largest blockchain event, with strong institutional and regulatory density. The 2026 edition focused significantly on MiCA implementation and its implications for tokenized assets — critical context for any European RWA issuance strategy.
→ RWA angle: MiCA regulatory framework, European institutional tokenization, cross-border compliance
→ www.parisblockchainweek.com/

Institutional Finance and Capital Markets Events

These are traditional finance conferences where tokenization has moved from fringe topic to main stage.

INSTITUTIONAL FINANCE
FT Digital Assets Summit 2026
May 13–14, 2026 ✅ completed · Aldersgate, London
Financial Times Live's flagship digital assets event, bringing together institutional leaders, innovators, and investors exploring the convergence of crypto and mainstream finance. Two days of keynotes, panels, and private networking covering RWA tokenization, digital asset allocation, and the evolving regulatory landscape. The FT brand brings in the TradFi executives who don't attend crypto-native events.
→ RWA angle: Where traditional asset managers first seriously engage with tokenization as a portfolio strategy
→ www.gdf.io/events/digital-assets-summit-2026/
INSTITUTIONAL FINANCE
Digital Asset Summit 2026 (Blockworks)
March 2026 ✅ completed · New York
Blockworks' institutional-facing event featuring 150+ speakers, 750+ companies, and 2,500 attendees. Positioned earlier in the year than Permissionless, DAS focuses on institutional strategy, capital allocation, and the infrastructure decisions being made by funds, banks, and asset managers. Consistently high-quality programming for the buy side.
→ RWA angle: Institutional capital allocation into digital assets, including tokenized RWAs
→ blockworks.co/events
INSTITUTIONAL FINANCE
Crypto Assets Conference 2026 (CAC26)
March 25, 2026 ✅ completed · Frankfurt School of Finance, Germany
European institutional conference at Frankfurt's premier finance school, covering tokenization, institutional crypto adoption, stablecoins, and regulatory frameworks under MiCA. The Frankfurt School venue signals the event's serious TradFi audience — this is not a retail crypto conference.
→ RWA angle: German and European institutional perspective on tokenization under MiCA
→ www.crypto-assets-conference.de/

Industry Verticals: Where RWA Is Showing Up Beyond Crypto

The most interesting development in the 2026 conference landscape is RWA showing up at industry-specific events that have nothing to do with crypto as their primary audience. Agtech conferences discussing supply chain tokenization. Real estate summits covering fractional ownership infrastructure. Commodity trading events adding blockchain tracks.

AGTECH / SUPPLY CHAIN
Watch: Agri-Finance and Agtech Conferences
Ongoing 2026 · Various
Agricultural finance conferences including the Global AgInvesting series, AgTech World Summit, and commodity trading events like the FT Commodities Global Summit are increasingly including tokenization and supply chain provenance sessions. As projects like GROW token, OriginTrail, and AgriDigital bring agricultural supply chains on-chain, the RWA audience extends into traditional agribusiness.
→ RWA angle: GROW token, OriginTrail, AgriDigital — supply chain tokenization as RWA use case
→ www.globalaginvesting.com/
REAL ESTATE
Watch: Proptech and Real Estate Capital Markets
Ongoing 2026 · Various
Real estate conferences including MIPIM, Urban Land Institute events, and proptech-specific gatherings are adding fractional ownership and tokenization sessions as platforms like RealT, Landshare, and Lofty gain traction. The real estate tokenization category is growing fast enough that mainstream property conferences can no longer ignore it.
→ RWA angle: Tokenized real estate — fractional ownership, liquidity infrastructure, regulatory clarity
→ www.mipim.com/
CARBON / ESG
Watch: Carbon Markets and ESG Finance Events
Ongoing 2026 · Various
With the EU Digital Product Passport mandate advancing and voluntary carbon markets under pressure to improve transparency, events like the Carbon Forum, Climate Finance Week, and ESG-focused investment conferences are featuring blockchain and on-chain carbon sessions. Toucan Protocol, Flowcarbon, and AirCarbon Exchange are all active in these spaces.
→ RWA angle: Toucan Protocol, Flowcarbon, AirCarbon — on-chain carbon credit tokenization
→ www.carbon-pulse.com/events/
GAMING / NFT ADJACENT
Rare Evo
Annual · Las Vegas (2025 edition held August 2025)
Primarily an NFT and Web3 gaming conference, Rare Evo is worth tracking for the RWA-adjacent audience it reaches. Gaming asset tokenization — in-game items, character NFTs, on-chain match logic — represents one of the most accessible on-ramps to blockchain participation for mainstream audiences. Projects like AdventureWurld and Yield Guild Games are building in this space. As NFT infrastructure converges with RWA standards (particularly around on-chain ownership records and programmable rewards), Rare Evo sits at a relevant intersection. Not a core RWA event, but one worth attending if gaming tokenization is part of your strategy.
→ RWA angle: Gaming asset tokenization, NFT/RWA infrastructure convergence, on-chain rewards and play-to-earn economies
→ rareevo.io/

How to Use This List

If you're new to the RWA space and want to get a foundational education quickly, TokenizeThis is the clearest starting point — it's built specifically for this audience and the programming reflects that. If you're looking for institutional relationships, RWA Day NYC or the FT Digital Assets Summit put you in rooms with allocators who are making real capital decisions. If you want the broadest network sweep, Consensus or TOKEN2049 Singapore give you scale.

The most underrated category is the industry vertical events — agtech, real estate, carbon. The signal density at a 500-person agricultural finance conference is often higher than a 20,000-person crypto event, because the people asking questions about tokenization at those events are the asset owners whose participation the sector needs most.
