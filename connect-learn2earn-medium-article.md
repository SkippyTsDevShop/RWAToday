# One Year of Learn2Earn: What 9,000 Participants and 300,000 Lessons Taught Us

**By Connect United**

---

A year ago, we asked a simple question: what if we rewarded people for learning about blockchain?

Not with hype. Not with promises of future riches. With real on-chain rewards, distributed regularly, for completing real lessons about technology that we genuinely believe is changing the world.

We recently celebrated one full year of Learn2Earn — 52 consecutive weeks of lessons, more than 9,000 participants, and over 300,000 individual lessons completed with rewards distributed on the WIN Blockchain. Every single one of those rewards being distributed is verifiable on-chain. No database. No spreadsheet. The chain is the record.

We didn't know it would look like this when we started. Here's what we learned.

---

## The Program

Every week, a lesson goes live on our Social Impact Platform at impact.connect.win. The topics have covered everything from Online Safety in Crypto to DeFi 101, Decentralization, How to DYOR, Bridging and Chains, On Ramping and Off Ramping, deep dives on Connect products and services as well as introduction to other partner projects from the decosystem (decentralized ecosystem) and their products and services.

But here's what makes Learn2Earn different from every other blockchain education program we've seen: **the lessons are taught by community members, for community members.**

We didn't hire a faculty. We didn't commission a course catalog. We empowered the people who were already in our community — people who had learned something, figured something out, crossed a threshold — to turn around and teach someone else. When a community member teaches a lesson, they have skin in the game. They've lived the confusion. They know where people get stuck. And they care whether the person in front of them actually understands.

That's a different kind of education. And the numbers show it works.

---

## What 300,000 Lessons Actually Means

We want to be honest about what this number represents — because numbers in blockchain are easy to inflate and we're not interested in doing that.

Every one of those 300,000 lesson completions represents a specific, verifiable action. A person showed up. They completed the lesson. A reward was distributed on the WIN Blockchain. That reward created a permanent on-chain record that can be verified by anyone, at any time, with no intermediary required.

That's not a completion in a database somewhere that we control. That's a completion on a blockchain that no one controls.

Over 9,000 unique participants have completed at least one lesson since we launched. Many have come back week after week, building a body of on-chain credentials that represents genuine, sustained engagement with blockchain education over time. A participant who has completed lessons across multiple weeks isn't just someone who clicked through a tutorial — they're someone who has used a wallet, received on-chain rewards, and navigated blockchain participation as a practical reality.

That's the threshold most crypto education programs never cross: from learning *about* blockchain to learning *on* blockchain.

---

## Why This Matters

The WIN Blockchain was built with a specific mission: to educate people about impact blockchain projects through Proof of Action — ventures using emerging and cutting-edge technologies to make a genuine difference in the world.

That's not a vague aspiration. It's a filter. The projects we introduce through Learn2Earn are the ones actually building — in agriculture, in environmental accountability, in community finance, in digital identity, in the tools that make blockchain genuinely useful for people who don't think of themselves as crypto investors.

One member on Trustpilot described the Learn2Earn platform as "genuinely informative — it clearly explains blockchain fundamentals, how nodes work, and what crypto is actually meant to be used for, not just speculation." That's exactly what we're going for.

The adoption challenge for blockchain isn't technical. The technology works. The challenge is human — getting people from "I've heard of this" to "I understand what it can do and I'm part of it." That gap is where most of the sector's good intentions disappear.

Connect United's Learn2Earn is our answer to that gap. Not a one-time explainer. Not a white paper. A weekly commitment, 52 weeks running, to meet people where they are and move with them one lesson at a time.

---

## What We Heard From Participants

The message that keeps coming back, in different words from different people across different countries, is some version of this: *"I didn't think I could understand this. Now I do."*

That's it. That's the whole program right there.

Blockchain has a reputation for being inaccessible — full of jargon, full of risk, full of people who seem to already know everything and aren't interested in explaining it. Learn2Earn is a deliberate counterpoint to that. Every week, someone who recently figured something out turns around and explains it to someone who hasn't yet. The knowledge moves forward. The community grows. The on-chain record of that growth is permanent.

Another Trustpilot reviewer put it in terms we couldn't have said better ourselves: Connect "creates a community that helps each other every step of the way. The lessons are engaging." That peer-to-peer dynamic — where the newest member today becomes someone else's guide next month — is the culture we've been deliberately building for 52 weeks.

Members are encouraged to collaborate, share ideas, and support one another's progress. As another community member wrote on Trustpilot: "That culture of unity and shared growth is rare and refreshing." We agree. And we intend to keep building it.
